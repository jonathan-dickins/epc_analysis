Charting fuel poverty in Cambridgeshire

I developed a fuel poverty proxy measure from EPC data and broke it down by MSOAs in Cambridgeshire.

The heatmap shows areas of relatively high and low potential fuel poverty, with rural MSOAs generally appearing to have a higher rate of fuel poverty.
